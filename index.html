I am a PHD student at Beijing International Center for Mathematical Research, Peking University. My advisor is Liang Xiao. Before that, I obtained my B.Sc. at Peking University.

My research interests lie in number theory and representation theory. I am especially interested in categorical Langlands program, and its application to number theory.
